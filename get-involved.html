§ 01 Choose how you help

Ways to make a difference.

Whether you have an afternoon or an institution behind you, there is a way to be part of the work. Every one of these reaches a woman in Khagaria or Bhagalpur who now knows something about her own body that she did not before.

01

Monthly Giving

Become a sustainer

Join a small circle of monthly supporters. Steady funding lets us plan camps, workshops, and cup distribution months ahead instead of week to week.

Set up monthly →
02

Volunteer

Hands-on, on the ground

Work alongside our field teams across Khagaria and Bhagalpur. You do not need a specific background — show up, listen, and learn from the community as much as you teach it.

Write to us · subject “Volunteer” →
03

Partner With Us

Institutions & CSR

Hospitals, colleges, companies and research bodies — like 10zyme and the University of Essex — who want to build something real with us, not just fund it.

Write to us · subject “Collaboration” →
04

Share Your Skills

Researchers & clinicians

Healthcare professionals, journalists, and researchers: lend your expertise to studies, CPD training, and storytelling that helps the work reach further.

Offer your expertise →
05

Advocate

Amplify our voice

Spread the word in your own network. Awareness is the first barrier we fight — help us reach the woman who has never heard of HPV, for a disease she does not know exists.

Tell someone →
06

One-Time Gift

Immediate impact

A single contribution toward cups, screenings, and workshops. Every rupee goes directly to the work — no overhead skim, no conditions.

Donate once →
