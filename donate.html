Donate

Every rupee goes directly to the work.

Behind every screening, every workshop, and every cup we distribute is a woman in Khagaria or Bhagalpur who now knows something about her own body that she did not before. That is what your donation makes possible.

What your support has already built

Since 2023, working across two districts with a small, committed team.

Where your gift goes

Choose an amount that feels right.

One woman

₹500

A menstrual cup and a one-to-one conversation for one woman. A single cup can last up to ten years.

A group

₹2,000

A full menstrual health workshop for a group of women, with cups for everyone who attends.

A village

₹5,000

A community health camp station for a day: blood pressure and diabetes screening, open to all, no cost.

A classroom

₹15,000

A complete school programme: hygiene and menstrual health education, and cups for an entire classroom of girls.

Any amount helps, and every amount is accounted for. Give what feels right to you using the details below.
